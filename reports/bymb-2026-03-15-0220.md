## 02 — Market Intelligence

### Mass-Market Installers

- **Visual Patterns:** Amateurish, primary colors (red/yellow), stock photos of cameras.
- **Messaging Patterns:** Price focus: 'Cheap', 'Offer', 'Same day installation'.
- **Strategic Weakness:** Zero brand equity, lack of formal compliance assurance.

### Tier-1 Industrial Integrators

- **Visual Patterns:** Cold, sterile, corporate grey/blue, overly complex diagrams.
- **Messaging Patterns:** Scale focus: 'Industrial grade', 'Government solutions', 'Enterprise monitoring'.
- **Strategic Weakness:** Inaccessible to SMEs, high friction sales process, impersonal service.
