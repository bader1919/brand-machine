## 01 — Brand Strategy

### Positioning Statement

- **Target:** Bahrain-based SMEs in retail, warehousing, and office management who are burdened by operational disruption from false alarms and anxious about regulatory compliance.
- **Frame Of Reference:** Technical security consultancy and precision system configuration.
- **Differentiation:** BYMB is the only provider that productizes security intelligence, offering a documented guarantee of less than 5% false alarms and MOI compliance audit-readiness, rather than merely selling hardware.
- **Reason To Believe:** Our proprietary 48-hour Smart Site Assessment, vendor-neutral SME Launchpad program, and PDPL-compliant documentation frameworks ensure a system that respects both privacy and safety.

### Unique Value Proposition

Precision-configured security that guarantees MOI compliance and eliminates false alarms, protecting your business from fines and operational chaos.

### Brand Archetype

**The Sage**

The Sage archetype fits because BYMB sells expertise, knowledge, and truth (finding the real source of alarms). The Ruler archetype supports the promise of control, stability, and compliance (providing order and structure to the client's environment).

### Brand Personality

- Meticulous
- Authoritative
- Pragmatic
- Transparent
- Proactive
- Analytical

### Messaging Pillars

- **** — Focus on the peace of mind that comes with MOI and PDPL compliance, ensuring the business is protected from legal risks.
- **** — Highlight the <5% false alarm promise. We don't just sell cameras; we sell the silence and focus that comes from accurate configuration.
- **** — Emphasize the delivery of professional documentation and data governance, moving security from a messy expense to a managed asset.
- **** — Reinforce that BYMB is an unbiased consultant working for the client's best interest, not a salesperson pushing specific hardware boxes.

### Tone of Voice

Professional, direct, and expertly precise. We speak like engineers who understand business risk—clear, concise, and devoid of fluff.

## 03 — Visual Identity

### Color Palette

| Role | Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|------|-----|-------|
| Primary | Midnight Navy | `#1A2744` | Primary text, dark backgrounds, hero sections, navigation, footers |
| Secondary | Dark Navy | `#243356` | Buttons, accents, cards on dark sections, secondary dark areas |
| Accent | Gold | `#C9A84C` | CTAs, premium highlights, dividers, icons, achievement markers — sparingly |
| Neutral | Cream | `#F8F5EF` | Warm backgrounds, feature sections, testimonials, pricing areas |
| Supporting | White | `#FFFFFF` | Default background, text on dark |
| Supporting | Mid Gray | `#B0AEA5` | Secondary text, captions, muted elements |
| Supporting | Soft Gold | `#F5EFE0` | Gradient D warm light cycle, subtle card backgrounds |
| Supporting | Brand Blue | `#4088C0` | Logo tagline ONLY — never used elsewhere in UI |
| Status | Success | `#2E7D5B` | Active, Paid, Complete, Confirmed |
| Status | Info | `#3B82C4` | Pending, In Progress, Processing |
| Status | Warning | `#D4922A` | Needs Review, Expiring Soon, Attention |
| Status | Danger | `#C44B4B` | Error, Cancelled, Expired, Overdue |

### Typography

- **Heading:** Poppins
- **Body:** Lora
- **Rationale:** Poppins (geometric sans) projects modernity and technical clarity — the Creator side. Lora (transitional serif) adds warmth, readability, and literary gravitas — the Sage side. Together they say 'smart AND approachable.'

### Imagery Direction

**Mood:** Confident, warm, purposeful. Images should feel like looking through the window of a high-end consultancy — real work happening, real technology in use, real results on screen. Never staged or stock-feeling.

- ✓ Real dashboards with actual data (anonymized)
- ✓ Genuine workspace environments
- ✓ Close-ups of quality hardware (Hikvision cameras, Zigbee devices)
- ✓ Architectural photography of Bahrain (warm-toned)
- ✓ Abstract geometric patterns using brand colors
- ✓ Before/after transformation comparisons
- ✕ Generic handshake photos
- ✕ Clipart-style illustrations
- ✕ Overly futuristic/sci-fi renders
- ✕ Blue-wash tech stock photos
- ✕ People looking at empty screens
- ✕ AI-generated faces that look uncanny
- ✕ Busy, cluttered compositions

### Non-Negotiable Rules

1. Logo must always include tagline at sizes 120px+ width
2. Gold never exceeds 10% of visual weight on any single page
3. Minimum contrast ratio 4.5:1 for all text
4. Poppins for headings, Lora for body — never reversed, never substituted
5. Status colors (#2E7D5B, #3B82C4, #D4922A, #C44B4B) are functional ONLY — never decorative
6. White space minimum 40% of any page or slide
7. Animated gradients: maximum ONE visible at a time, never on buttons
8. Photography must be warm-toned — no blue-cast, no cold lighting
9. Every page/slide must include the gold accent bar somewhere
10. Brand Blue (#4088C0) is ONLY for the logo tagline — never in UI
